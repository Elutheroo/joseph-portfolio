Theo’s Bakery

Designing a bakery e-commerce platform from research to high-fidelity mockups

Overview

This project was an end-to-end UX/UI design process for a bakery's e-commerce platform, including user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design.

Problem & Goals

Problem: The bakery needed an online platform that was both visually appealing and easy for customers to place orders.

Goals: Create a responsive, visually engaging, and user-friendly website with a seamless order process.

Duration

3 Weeks

Role

UX/UI Designer

Process

  • Conducted user interviews to understand ordering habits.
  • Created personas and user flows.
  • Developed wireframes and tested them with target users.
  • Designed high-fidelity prototypes in Figma and prepared for handoff.

Results

The design improved online ordering efficiency, increased visual appeal, and boosted customer satisfaction.

Tools Used

Figma, Miro, Google Forms
