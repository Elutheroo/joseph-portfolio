Theo’s Bakery

A mobile app for ordering fresh bakery products from home.

Project Overview

Duration: Jan 31, 2022 – Mar 2, 2022

The Problem: Customers cannot conveniently order bakery items from home.

The Goal: Enable easy ordering and delivery for fresh products.

My Role: UX Designer & Researcher

Persona

Name: Daniel

Age: 18

Goals: Spend more time studying, get bakery products without visiting the bakery.

Frustrations: Bakery visits waste study time, transportation costs are high.

Accessibility Considerations

  • High-contrast color palette for readability
  • Dark mode option for comfort
  • Clear, simple language for better comprehension

Impact & Learnings

The app offers a time-saving and convenient alternative for customers, increasing accessibility and potential sales for the bakery.

What I Learned: Strengthened empathy for users and honed skills in designing products for real-world problems.
