Study Room

Role: UX/UI Designer | Duration: 2 Months

Tools: Figma, Notion

The Challenge

Students needed a lightweight, collaborative space to share notes, schedule study sessions, and find peers for group work. Existing solutions were fragmented and lacked a focused workflow.

Approach

  1. Interviewed students and tutors to understand collaboration pain points.
  2. Built low-fidelity flows to validate core features: rooms, shared notes, and quick invites.
  3. Iterated on high-fidelity screens and prototype-tested with users.

Outcome

The Study Room prototype improved clarity around finding study groups and reduced time-to-first-collaboration during usability tests.
