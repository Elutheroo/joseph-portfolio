Study Room

A collaborative learning app to help students find peers, share notes, and schedule study sessions.

Quick Facts

Role: UX/UI Designer

Duration: 2 Months

Tools: Figma, Notion

View Prototype

Project Overview

Study Room is a demo project that helps students discover peers, share notes, and coordinate sessions. The goal was to create a lightweight app with a clear, friendly interface that reduced friction for joining and organizing study groups.

My Process

A research-driven approach tailored to student workflows, prioritizing quick discovery and low-friction collaboration.

01

User Research

Conducted interviews and contextual inquiry with students to uncover common collaboration patterns and pain points. Synthesized findings into personas and key user journeys.

02

Design & Validation

Iterated on low-fidelity flows then ran moderated usability tests on high-fidelity prototypes. Refined navigation and onboarding to reduce first-time confusion.

Wireframes and UI

Impact and Learning

The prototype demonstrated improved first-time success rates in key tasks (finding peers, creating sessions). Prioritizing onboarding and discoverability increased user confidence in early tests.

Outcome

A concise, testable prototype with four polished screens and a wireframe set that showcases interaction patterns for future development.
