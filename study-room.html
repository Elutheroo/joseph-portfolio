Study Room

A collaborative learning app to help students find peers, share notes, and schedule study sessions.

Role: UX/UI Designer • Duration: 2 Months • Tools: Figma, Notion

The Challenge

Students needed a lightweight, collaborative space to share notes, schedule study sessions, and find peers for group work. Existing solutions were fragmented and lacked a focused workflow.

01

Research

Interviewed students and tutors to understand key pain points and priorities.

02

Design & Test

Built low-fidelity flows and iterated on high-fidelity prototypes with usability testing.
