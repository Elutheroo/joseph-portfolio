Theo’s Bakery

Role: UX/UI Designer | Duration: 3 Months

Tools: Figma, Adobe XD

Overview

Theo’s Bakery needed an e-commerce platform that was easy to navigate and showcased their bakery products beautifully. The aim was to design a clean, modern interface that could boost online sales.

Process

  • Conducted competitor analysis and identified gaps in bakery e-commerce platforms.
  • Created wireframes focusing on user-friendly navigation and appealing product displays.
  • Developed high-fidelity mockups with a warm, inviting color scheme.
  • Built a prototype in Figma for usability testing and iteration.

Results

The final design provided a streamlined purchase journey, improved product visibility, and increased brand trust through consistent design elements.
