Theo’s Bakery

A warm, inviting e-commerce experience focused on product visibility and streamlined purchase flows.

Quick Facts

Role: UX/UI Designer

Duration: 3 Months

Tools: Figma, Adobe XD

Overview

Theo’s Bakery needed an e-commerce platform that was easy to navigate and showcased their bakery products beautifully. The aim was to design a clean, modern interface that could boost online sales.

01

Research & Analysis

Competitor review and gap analysis shaped the product and navigation decisions.

02

Wireframes & Mockups

Focused on product discovery and simple purchase paths with clear CTAs.

Results

Streamlined purchase journey and improved product visibility.
